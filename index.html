About Me

The past several years I've been searching for a career that was intellectually stimulating, financially secure and allowed me to help the world at large. About a year ago, I discovered my passion for Web Development. I started on Codecademy and then was accepted to the Lambda School. In my time there I learned modern Web Development techniques and Computer Science basics.

I love to design and implement a sleek and easily accessible UI/UX. I consider myself a Fullstack Web Developer, but I particularly love working on the front end, starting with a blank canvas and turning it into a beautiful and fully functional web application.
